Imposter Online – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

Imposter Online ("the App", "we", "us") is a multiplayer party game developed by Aakash Goradia. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • A unique anonymous User ID generated automatically by Firebase Authentication — no name, email, or phone number required.
  • A username you choose (3–16 characters).
  • An emoji avatar you select.
  • A randomly generated friend code.

Gameplay Data

  • Words submitted and votes cast during gameplay.
  • Game settings and preferences (timer, number of imposters, categories).
  • Game session data (game codes, player lists, game results).

Social Features Data

  • Friend lists (stored as user ID references).
  • Friend requests sent and received.
  • Game invitations sent and received.
  • In-game chat messages and direct messages between friends.

Technical Data

  • Online/offline status and last seen timestamp.
  • Device identifier used by Firebase for authentication and connection management.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Real names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data or physical addresses
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Health, fitness, or biometric data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Browsing history or data from other apps
  • Advertising identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

All collected data is used solely for App functionality:

  • Authentication: To create and maintain your anonymous account.
  • Gameplay: To facilitate real-time multiplayer games (role assignment, word submission, voting, results).
  • Social Features: To enable friend lists, friend requests, game invitations, and direct messaging.
  • Online Status: To show friends whether you are currently active.
  • Content Moderation: Usernames and chat messages are filtered for profanity before being stored.

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication) on Google Cloud infrastructure, with encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest.

We retain your data only for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, the following is permanently removed:

  • Your user profile (username, avatar, friend code)
  • Your friend list and friend requests
  • Your game invitations and DM notifications
  • Your username and friend code reservations
  • Your Firebase Authentication account

Game session data (messages, votes, submitted words) from completed games is part of a shared game record and is not individually deleted upon account deletion.

5. Children's Privacy

Imposter Online does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App uses anonymous authentication and requires no real personal information. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses only one third-party service:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore Database) — subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not integrate any advertising networks, analytics platforms, social media SDKs, or other third-party services.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Delete Your Account: Permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Settings screen in the App.
  • Change Your Username: Update your username at any time from Settings.
  • Remove Friends: Remove any friend from your list at any time.
  • Sign Out: Sign out of your account at any time from Settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
